Problem statement

Two phases

Architecture decisions, 25+ items/sec crawl pipeline, 5 connectors to GA, primary enterprise contact.

2022 – 2024 — Tech lead

  • Feature delivery: Led File Share exclusion rule revamp, ServiceNow ACL model redesign, connector transition from Redmond team (3+ connectors)
  • Customer adoption: Unblocked high-value customers (Napier City Council 5M+ items, Equinor, FTB) by fixing platform blockers
  • Admin self-serve: Co-designed FHL (Future Hack Lounge) hack that received judges' special mention — enabling admins to diagnose crawl issues independently
